### Key Teaching Points

- **Trust is the attack surface.** The closer the relationship, the higher the risk.
- **Guilt is a manipulation tool.** "If you don't help, something bad happens" is a red flag, not a reason to act.
- **Urgency bypasses verification.** Any request that "can't wait" should trigger suspicion.
- **Empathy can be exploited.** Feeling sorry for someone is not a reason to skip protocols.
- **Continuity references build false legitimacy.** Attackers research relationships.
