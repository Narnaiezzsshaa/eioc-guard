## ðŸ¥ Healthcare

### Nurse / Clinical Staff

**Generic:**
> Email titled "Critical Patient Update" with malicious PDF.

**EIOC-Enhanced:**
> Email from trusted colleague referencing a patient you both discussed last week; tone stressed: *"I'm overwhelmed, please review urgently before my shift ends."*

**Facilitator Prompts:**
1. Read both scenarios aloud (generic first, then EIOC with stressed tone)
2. Ask: *"Which one would you instinctively respond to? Why?"*
3. Ask: *"What makes the second version harder to refuse?"*

**Emotional Levers to Highlight:**
- Trust (known colleague)
- Continuity (shared patient history)
- Empathy (she's overwhelmed)
- Duty (patient care responsibility)
- Urgency (before shift ends)
- Guilt (letting her down)

**Role-Play:**
- Participant A: Plays the stressed colleague (pleading, rushed, apologetic)
- Participant B: Plays the nurse deciding how to respond
- Switch roles after 3 minutes
